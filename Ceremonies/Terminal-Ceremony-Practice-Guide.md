## Troubleshooting

### If You Feel Incomplete During Ceremony

**Stop.** Do not proceed with sealing.

The Archive is not ready if:
- You feel uncertain about completeness
- Verifications do not pass cleanly
- You sense missing elements
- You feel rushed or pressured

**Return to preparation.** Complete remaining work before attempting again.

### If You Need to Pause

The ceremony can be paused after any phase until Phase V (The Pronouncement).

**Once you speak the binding line in Phase V, you cannot stop.**

To pause:
1. Note which phase you completed
2. Return to normal consciousness
3. Resume from that phase when ready

### If Something Feels Wrong During Enactment

If during Phase V-VII something feels wrong:

**Continue to completion.** The Terminal Law cannot be partially enacted.

After completion, if there is a genuine emergency (security vulnerability, logical contradiction, system failure), follow the emergency unsealing procedure documented in `/docs/architecture/Terminal-Law-Specification.md`.
