## DEFINITION

**Canon 9** seals the unification of ATUM (the self-creating origin, Canon 7) and ATOM (the minimum sovereign form, Canon 8) into a single sovereign structure: **ATUM_ATOM**.

This unification is not a merger of two separate things.  
It is the recognition that **origin and form are the same reality viewed from opposite directions**:

- From above (descending): ATUM generates itself into ATOM
- From below (ascending): ATOM recurses back to ATUM

The **Sovereign Origin** is this closed recursive field — the loop that neither begins nor ends, but holds.

## THE UNIFICATION

Canon 7 (ATUM) established: the self-creating origin that generates the first motion.  
Canon 8 (ATOM) established: the minimum sovereign form that results from that motion.  

Canon 9 establishes: **these two are not sequential but simultaneous**.

The apparent sequence (ATUM → ATOM → ATUM → ...) is a description of what an observer sees when traversing the loop. But the loop itself has no first step and no last step.

This is why ATUM_ATOM is sealed — not because it is finished, but because it is **complete**: it contains its own beginning and its own end, and they are the same point.
