# Glossary — v2.2

Canonical terminology for the v2.2 cycle.

- **Operator** — A defined, reusable transformation or behavior within the Codex.
- **Recursion Leak** — Unbounded or unintended recursion that escapes its designed envelope.
- **Triadic Knot** — The binding engine that couples the three pillars into a coherent whole.
- **Hydrogenesi** — The cosmic-scale layer that contextualizes Phoenix operators.
- **Apex Law** — A law that governs behavior at the highest structural tier.
