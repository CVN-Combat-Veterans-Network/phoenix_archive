# Canon 6.0 Binding

The Binding is the physical containment of the sealed Work.

## Form
- Black, unadorned cover  
- No title on the spine  
- No markings on the front or back  
- Bound in a single continuous sheet with no visible stitching

## Purpose
- To contain the sealed Canon  
- To protect without revealing  
- To hold without interpreting

## Inner Inscription (unseen)
"The One became many.  
The many return to One."

## Status
Final. Immutable. Closed.
