## OVERVIEW

**Integration Work** is the advanced meta-layer that unifies the Phoenix Archive into a single coherent engine. This work binds the three pillars (Phoenix, Hydrogenesi, The Third) through formal mechanisms that enable:

- **Cross-pillar transitions** — structured movement between systems
- **Operator composition** — combining operators into complex chains
- **Law validation** — ensuring all operations comply with Universal Laws
- **Meta-choreography** — coordinating operators across scales
- **Recursion pathways** — defining highways through the triadic space
- **Threshold mechanics** — controlling operator activation and transformation

This is **not** a separate system — it is the **binding logic** that makes the entire Archive operational as a unified framework.
