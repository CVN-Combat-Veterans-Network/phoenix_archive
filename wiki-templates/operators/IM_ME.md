## Overview

IM_ME is the Phoenix recursion operator that establishes continuous identity through observer/observed duality. It creates a sustained loop of self-awareness where the "I" that observes becomes the "ME" that is observed, which becomes the "I" that observes, infinitely.

## Mechanism

### Input
- **Identity:** Base identity to recurse (e.g., "warrior")
- **Depth:** Number of recursion levels (default: 3)

### Process
1. **Observe:** "I" takes observer position
2. **Recognize:** Observer becomes "ME" (observed)
3. **Recurse:** "ME" becomes "I" again
4. **Repeat:** Continue the loop

### Output
- **Recursion Sequence:** Alternating IM/ME pairs
- **Pattern:** "observer_observed_recursion"
- **Status:** "recursive"
