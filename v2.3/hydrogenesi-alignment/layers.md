## DEFINITION

This document provides **canonical definitions** of all **14 Hydrogenesi Layers** ‚Äî the vertical stratification system that organizes cosmic operators, instruments, and energy flows from foundational tension to sovereign apex structures.

Each layer specifies:
- **Layer Number & Name**
- **Cosmic Function** ‚Äî What role this stratum serves in universal structure
- **Bound Instruments** ‚Äî Which detection/measurement tools operate at this layer
- **Energy State** ‚Äî Characteristic energy configuration (tension, compression, binding, etc.)
- **Structural Role** ‚Äî Position in foundation ‚Üí transition ‚Üí apex hierarchy
- **Operator Bindings** ‚Äî Which operators activate at this stratum
- **Cross-Layer Interactions** ‚Äî How this layer interfaces with adjacent strata

**These definitions are invariant.** All cosmic processes must respect layer boundaries.

## LAYER 1: FOUNDATION

### **Name:** Foundation  
### **Cosmic Function:** Convergence/Divergence/Stabilization/Destabilization

**Description:**  
Layer 1 is the **foundational tension field** where opposing cosmic forces (convergence ‚Üî divergence) establish the primordial substrate for all higher structures. This is the **entry point** for cosmic energy ‚Äî no pattern can form without first manifesting as tension at Layer 1.

### Bound Instruments
- **TensionGauge** ‚Äî Detects convergence/divergence force magnitudes
- **StabilizerLens** ‚Äî Measures stabilization vs. destabilization tendencies
- **DivergenceTracker** ‚Äî Maps divergent flow vectors
- **ConvergenceMap** ‚Äî Identifies convergence nodes

### Energy State
**Tension Polarity:** Unresolved binary opposition (A ‚Üî B)  
**Energy Configuration:** High potential, unstable equilibrium  
**Stability:** Minimal (requires Layer 2+ to achieve coherence)

### Structural Role
**Position:** Base of cosmic stack  
**Function:** Establish tension pairs that seed all higher formations  
**Ascension Requirement:** Must achieve stabilization before energy can rise to Layer 2

### Operator Bindings
- **FirstBinding** ‚Äî Transforms binary tension into triadic structure (A + S + B)
- **StabilizerExtraction** ‚Äî Inverse operation: exposes underlying tension

### Cross-Layer Interactions
- **‚Üí Layer 2:** Stabilized tension patterns propagate upward as resonance
- **‚Üê Layer 0:** (None ‚Äî Layer 1 is cosmic base)

**Example:**  
Dark matter field exhibits convergence (gravitational collapse) vs. divergence (cosmic expansion). TensionGauge measures the opposition. FirstBinding introduces stabilizer (angular momentum), forming stable rotation curve. Energy can now ascend to Layer 2 (Resonance).

### Operator Bindings

### Cross-Layer Interactions
