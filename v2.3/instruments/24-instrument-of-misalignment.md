## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Misalignment** disrupts unified direction and restores directional diversity. It is the complementary temporal instrument to Alignment, enabling exploration through directional freedom and preventing narrow focus.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (exploring alternatives, creative divergence) and **macro-scale** (magnetic randomization, trajectory scatter), creating adaptive flexibility through directional variety.
