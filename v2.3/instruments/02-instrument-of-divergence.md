## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Divergence** disperses a unified source into multiple distinct paths, energies, or identities. It is the complementary instrument to Convergence, enabling differentiation and exploration.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (identity differentiation) and **macro-scale** (cosmic expansion), allowing sovereign structures to extend and multiply.
