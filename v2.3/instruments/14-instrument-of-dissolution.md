## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Dissolution** transforms fixed, rigid structures back into fluid, adaptive states. It is the complementary formation instrument to Crystallization, enabling transformation by releasing locked patterns and restoring possibility.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (breaking limiting identities, habit dissolution) and **macro-scale** (melting, structural breakdown), marking the transition from being back to becoming.
