## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Deceleration** reduces velocity and dissipates momentum, transforming rapid kinetic flows into controlled, manageable motion. It is the complementary kinetic instrument to Acceleration, preventing crashes and enabling safe transitions.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (burnout prevention, skill refinement) and **macro-scale** (orbital capture, safe landing), creating controlled velocity reduction.
