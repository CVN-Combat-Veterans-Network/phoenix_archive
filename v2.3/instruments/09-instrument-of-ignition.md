## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Ignition** initiates self-sustaining energy release through threshold crossing. It is the foundational kinetic instrument that transforms potential energy into cascading kinetic transformation, marking the beginning of autonomous process.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (breakthrough moments, habit formation) and **macro-scale** (stellar ignition, chain reactions), creating irreversible transformation through energy release.
