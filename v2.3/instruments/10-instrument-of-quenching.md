## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Quenching** arrests self-sustaining processes and stops energy cascades. It is the complementary kinetic instrument to Ignition, preventing runaway reactions and enabling controlled termination of autonomous processes.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (stopping harmful habits, ending cycles) and **macro-scale** (stellar death, reaction cessation), creating controlled endings through energy removal.
