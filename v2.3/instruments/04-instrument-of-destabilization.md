## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Destabilization** disrupts fixed, rigid structures and returns them to dynamic, adaptive states. It is the necessary counterbalance to stabilization, preventing ossification and enabling transformation.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (breaking limiting patterns) and **macro-scale** (disrupting locked structures), allowing evolution and renewal.
