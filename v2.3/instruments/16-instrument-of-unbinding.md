## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Unbinding** separates unified structures into independent elements. It is the complementary formation instrument to Binding, enabling transformation by releasing connections and restoring autonomy to bound elements.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (ending relationships, concept separation) and **macro-scale** (molecular dissociation, gravitational escape), creating freedom through disconnection.
