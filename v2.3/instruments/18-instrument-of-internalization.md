## DEFINITION

The **Instrument of Internalization** integrates external experiences into internal structure. It is the complementary reciprocal instrument to Projection, enabling growth through absorption and transformation of external inputs into internal resources.

This instrument operates at both **micro-scale** (learning, emotional integration) and **macro-scale** (energy absorption, matter accretion), revealing how external reality shapes internal states.
