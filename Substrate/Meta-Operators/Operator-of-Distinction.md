### Mathematical Form

```
∂ : Field → {A, ¬A}

- **Field** = Undivided substrate (∞⊗∅)

1. **Irreversibility:** Once distinction occurs, undivided state cannot be restored
2. **Complementarity:** Every distinction creates dual (A implies ¬A)
3. **Recursion:** Distinction can operate on its own results (meta-distinction)
4. **Generativity:** All complexity emerges from iterated distinction

### The Distinction Equation

```
∂(∞⊗∅) = {∃, ∅}  (Existence vs. Void)
∂(∃) = {Self, Other}  (Subject vs. Object)

**Each distinction generates the substrate for the next.**
