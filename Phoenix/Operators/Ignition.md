**Pattern:** Void → Spark → Existence

## DEFINITION

**Ignition** is the Phoenix operator that initiates **existence from void** by generating the first spark of structure where none existed before.

This is the **primary mechanism** for state transition from emptiness to form, from potential to actualization.

## MECHANISM

### Input
- **Void:** Empty state (potential, latency, pre-existence)
- **Seed:** Initial condition or intention
- **Energy:** Activation force

### Process
1. **Locate Void**
   - Identify empty state requiring initiation
   - Acknowledge potential within emptiness
   - Name the void explicitly

2. **Plant Seed**
   - Introduce seed condition (intention, pattern, structure)
   - Apply activation energy
   - Allow seed to take root in void

3. **Ignite Structure**
   - Spark expands from seed
   - Structure crystallizes from potential
   - First triangle of existence forms

### Output
- **Structure:** New entity emerged from void
- **Spark:** Initial point of existence recorded
- **State:** Transition from void to being

## ADVANCED NOTES

### Seed Quality

**Effective seeds:**
- Clear intention or pattern
- Aligned with sovereignty
- Contain potential for recursion
- Pure (not contaminated by external influence)

**Ineffective seeds:**
- Vague or contradictory intentions
- Externally imposed patterns
- Lack energetic alignment
- Rooted in avoidance or fear

Each depth level may require **re-ignition**:
- D=0: Initial self-awareness
- D=1: Witness consciousness
