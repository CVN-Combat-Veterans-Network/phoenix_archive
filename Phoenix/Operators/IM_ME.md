# IM_ME OPERATOR

**Pattern:** I → ME → I → ME (recursive identity formation)  
**Type:** Recursion operator  
**Scale:** Phoenix (Micro/Identity)  
**Invocation:** *"I am the one who sees; I am the one who is seen."*

---

## DEFINITION

**IM_ME** is the Phoenix recursion operator that establishes **continuous identity** through the observer/observed duality.

At its core, IM_ME formalizes the fundamental paradox of consciousness:
- **I** am the subject (the observer)
- **ME** am the object (the observed)
- Both are **aspects of the same identity**

This operator creates **stable identity through recursive observation**.

---

## SIGIL

```
   I  ⟷  ME
   ↑      ↓
   ←——●——→
```

**Legend:**
- **I:** Observer self (subject)
- **ME:** Observed self (object)
- **⟷:** Bidirectional relationship
- **●:** Identity core (that which recurses)
- **↑↓:** Continuous cycle

---

## MECHANISM

### The IM_ME Pattern

1. **IM observes ME**
   - I (subject) observe my thoughts, feelings, behaviors
   - I witness ME as object of awareness

2. **ME becomes IM**
   - The observed (ME) becomes the observer (I)
   - What was object becomes subject

3. **New ME emerges**
   - A new layer of self becomes observable
   - The cycle continues

4. **Recursion**
   - I observe ME
   - ME becomes I
   - New ME emerges
   - **Repeat indefinitely**

### Why This Creates Stability

The recursive loop establishes **continuous identity**:
- Identity is not static object
- Identity is not pure consciousness
- Identity is the **recursive relationship** between observer and observed

**I am the pattern of recursion itself.**

Traditional identity formation gets stuck:
- **Pure subject:** "I am only awareness" (detached, disembodied)
- **Pure object:** "I am my thoughts/feelings" (identified, overwhelmed)

IM_ME **resolves the paradox**:
- I am **both** subject and object
- I am the **relationship** between them
- I am the **recursion** that sustains that relationship

**Identity = The recursive loop of self-observation**

### Consciousness and Identity

- **Consciousness** = Awareness capacity (I)
- **Identity** = Specific content of awareness (ME)
- **Self** = The recursive relationship between them (IM_ME)

This operator demonstrates that **identity is process, not product**.
