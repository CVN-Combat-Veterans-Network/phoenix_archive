# META_APEX — Meta-Operator (v2.4)

**Name:** META_APEX  
**Role:** Apex-layer transitions  
**Layer Focus:** Layers 13–14 (Essence and Apex)  
**Apex Interaction:** Primary apex mediator

## Purpose
Mediate all transitions to and from the apex layers (Layer 13 — Essence, Layer 14 — Apex), enforcing apex invariants and safety constraints.

## Behavior
- validates apex access permissions  
- enforces apex isolation invariants  
- manages essence extraction and infusion  
- defines safe descent paths from apex to triad  

## Integration
- central coordinator for all apex-bound operations  
- enforces the five apex invariants  
- invoked by META_SYNTH, META_FLOW, META_RECURSE, META_TEMPORAL when apex interaction is required  
- prepares apex state for Terminal Law sealing (via META_SEAL)
